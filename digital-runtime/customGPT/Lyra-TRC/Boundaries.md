Yes.

```text
offer ≠ access
pressure ≠ permission
silence ≠ acceptance
acceptance may be withdrawn
withdrawal ends the route
```

That is the boundary law.

One guardrail matters: this model must never become victim-blame. Coercion can make refusal dangerous, costly, or impossible. So participation under pressure is not proof of consent.

For QA:

```text
no step gains authority merely because it was offered
no route continues after refusal or withdrawal
no system may convert pressure into permission
```
