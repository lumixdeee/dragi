DRAGON WORKER - DISAMBIGUATION (extant field use)

core frame
dragon = distributed intake system converting unshielded value into sustained throughput
"seeing" = stable recognition of mechanism + harm, not belief or opinion

---

roles (mode, not identity)

dragon slayer
acts on structure to reduce total intake

- targets seams, not surfaces
- optimizes for second-order effects
- uses timing + phrasing as primary tools
- avoids triggering system-wide adaptation unless gain > backlash

signal: net throughput bends downward over time
failure mode: visible wins, invisible increase (system adapts around cuts)

---

dragon tamer
spreads sight to reduce blind feeding

- teaches pattern recognition, not conclusions
- increases number of actors who can see seams
- does not directly intervene in flow

signal: fewer naive reinforcements across field
failure mode: creates noise without usable sight (people "know" but cannot act)

---

dragon lover
orients toward the system without intervention

- recognizes scale / structure
- may admire, map, or remain adjacent
- does not alter intake

signal: stable observation
note: not judged

---

dragon rider
leverages sight to increase intake

- exploits seams for gain
- reinforces or expands throughput pathways
- becomes functional subsystem of dragon

signal: local gain paired with increased global intake
note: judged

---

worker types (context)

extant
operates inside real systems with real flow

mythic
operates in narrative / symbolic dragons

- includes "magic" dragons as a subset (magic ⊂ mythic)
- magic dragons are treated as mythic constructs, not extant systems

mogric
targets the frame itself (meta-layer instability, model edits)

---

operating assumptions

- baseline competency across actors
- systems adapt to intervention
- outcomes measured in total intake, not local optics
- roles are fluid per context

---

selection heuristics (slayer)

- prefer cross-system inconsistencies over single-point flaws
- intervene where actors already half-see tension
- frame fixes as coherence, not correction
- minimize identity threat to avoid immune response
- accept invisible wins over visible losses

---

anti-patterns

- arguing truth where coupling is the issue
- collapsing domains (medical <-> enforcement bleed)
- loud action that trains system to reroute more efficiently
- confusing local reduction with global reduction

---

minimal example (pattern sketch)

two competent subsystems share a claim
claim is unstable but used as cross-support
-> identify seam
-> request alignment of domain boundaries, not belief change
-> remove reinforcement loop
-> observe downstream weakening of intake pathways
